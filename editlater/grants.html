Conference Grant Application

The grant application to attend CS22 is now closed (deadline was 01 March 2024).

→Please fill this form if you wish to submit a conference grant application for funding to attend CS22.←

The grants will cover the early bird registration fee and 50% of the on-campus housing costs for in-person participants. They will not cover travel fees (e.g., plane/train tickets). Note that you must still submit an abstract through the general abstract submission form. If you apply for a grant, please wait until our official decision before registering for CS22, as grantees will have access to a unique registration code.

Deadline is 01 March 2024.
