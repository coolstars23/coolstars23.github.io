Episode XXII

SAN DIEGO


It is a time of scientific discovery throughout the Galaxy. Since Cool Stars 21, data from the James Webb Space Telescope is flowing, the Rubin Observatory Legacy Survey of Space and Time has seen first-light, and Euclid has launched.

Major advances in theoretical and computational astrophysics have occurred. Now, equipped with the ultimate scientific tools, astrophysicists have enough power to unlock the secrets of the Universe.

There is much to rejoice about, however, something is brewing in San Diego. A small group of astronomers have been planning something for 2024....
