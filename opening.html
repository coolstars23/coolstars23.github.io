SAN DIEGO

In a galaxy not so far away, Elan Tusk has amassed the resources of the entire Sol system. Meanwhile, CheatGPT has become ubiquitous in everyday life, helping people with everything from writing emails to ordering groceries...and writing proposals to finishing assignments.

The James Webb Space Telescope and Euclid have launched and are producing stunning images and spectra of cool stars, brown dwarfs, and giant exoplanets. The Rubin Legacy Survey of Space and Time is on the horizon, with first light later this year...or next.

It is a time of great scientific prosperity, however, something is about to take place in San Diego La Jolla. A rogue group of astrophysicists and astronomers who care only about the coolest stars (and brown dwarfs and planets) are about to overwhelm the Sol system with a deluge of talks, plots, posters, science, and most importantly, KARAOKE....
